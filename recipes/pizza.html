Pizza

Description

This recipe will show you how to make authentic Pizza Napolitana For the best results and quality use only fresh ingredients and follow the steps! 2-3 servings

Ingredients

  • 200g Canned or fresh San Marzano tomatos
  • 1 tbs Olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tbs Salt
  • 500 gr Flour type 0
  • 1/2 ts sugar
  • Black Pepper
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 gr dry yeast
  • 1 ts oregano
  • 2 leafs of basil
  • 150 gr Mozarella cheese
  1. Pour one cup of sparkling water into the big bowl and add 1 tbs of salt, then wisk it together until salt is desolved
  2. Add a little bit flour, it should look like dough for pancakes, then wisk it together. Meanwhile in the small cup pour a little bit of water and add yeasts. Afterwards add water with yeasts into the bowl with flour.
  3. Add the rest of a flour, while wisking the mixture in the bowl until your dough is ready, then put it covered, in warm place so it can grow.
  4. Every 20-30 min knead the dough a little bit with your hands
  5. Now we can start preparing our sauce. In the small pan we heat up 1 tbs of Olive oil and when the temperature is right we add grounded garlic
  6. After few seconds we add tomatoes which have to be blended before. Now we can add how much Black pepper we would like, 1/2 tbs of salt, 1/2 ts of sugar, 1 ts of oregano and 2 leafs of basil
  7. When everything is added and mixed together we can put cover on and leave it on slow cook for 10-15 min. Check your sauce every 5 min and wisk it a little bit with your spoon
  8. Preheat your pizza oven on maximum temperature
  9. Now when we have both our sauce and our dough ready we can start to make pizza. By using your hands slighlt stretch and knead your dough to make it thinner and shapier.
  10. If we are satisfied with shape of our pizza we can put our sauce and on top of it Mozarella cheese
  11. Gently push your pizza to the oven. Use flour on the bottom of your dough so the pizza can slid easier
